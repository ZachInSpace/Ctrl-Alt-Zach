Canadian Cybersecurity Advocacy

Contact Parliament About Bill C-22

Bill C-22, the Lawful Access Act, 2026, includes a Part 2 framework dealing with technical capabilities for authorized access to information. This page provides a customizable email template for Canadians who want Parliament to consider the cybersecurity and national-security risks of compelled privileged-access capabilities.

Use the Email Template Find Your MP

The Core Concern

Lawful authority does not eliminate technical risk

New attack surface

Privileged-access mechanisms, interfaces, and technical capabilities can become high-value targets for criminals, malicious insiders, and foreign intelligence services.

Security is not user-selective

A technical capability designed for authorized use still exists inside the same systems defenders must protect from unauthorized use.

Precedent matters

The Athens Affair, the escape and misuse of EternalBlue, and modern telecom intrusions associated with Salt Typhoon all demonstrate why privileged capabilities and communications infrastructure deserve exceptional security scrutiny.
